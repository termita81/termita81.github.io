40th Anniversary conference

The 40th Anniversary Conference and Celebration was held Saturday May 13. A fantastic turnout of over 200 volunteers, students and presenters. If you attended, we do hope that you enjoyed the day and took home with you some practical tips for your next tutoring session. More info and photos here.

Tutor Training

Tutor training is run throughout the year. Our final metropolitan training course for 2017 will begin in October. If you are interested in being a volunteer with the Program please submit an application so you can be invited to our next course.


Read Write Now student to feature on SBS Insight program

As you well know, it is rare for adult literacy to be in the spotlight as the shame and stigma of poor literacy result in it being covered up.
The TV program Insight, hosted by Jennie Brockie will focus on this issue in the program titled Reading Between the Lines (aired Tuesday 23rd August), and the really exciting news is that a Read Write Now student is a guest on the program. More information here.

Read Write Now is a volunteer group that was set up in Western Australia in 1977. Volunteer tutors provide free one-to-one assistance to adults wanting to improve their reading, writing, spelling, maths and/or IT skills.

Did you know that almost half of the Australian population struggles with everyday practical literacy skills? That's why newspapers are written for the reading level of 10-11 year olds.
